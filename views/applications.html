Examples where AI has been implemented

  • Self Driving Cars
    The AI driving a car is constantly taking in input, making predictions, and has access to a very large set of data, so it is able to adapt to the continuously changing driving conditions.
  • Speech Recognition
    AI is also used to translate languages. It needs to be able to differentiate between accents and must have access to all the vocabulary of multiple languages.
  • Computers that Play Games
    Computers have been known to constantly beat world champions at games like chest, checkers, and even video games by planning hundreds or even thousand of moves ahead.
  • Coloring Black and White Videos
    AI has greatly accelerated the time it takes to color black and white films by recognizing patterns of already colored video and applying them accordingly.
